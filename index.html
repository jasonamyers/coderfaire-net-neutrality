Timeline

In the beginning...

  • Oct 2002 - DSL and Cable are "special"; FCC deregulates cable

Timeline

2005

  • Mar 2005 - FCC fines Madison River - Blocking VoIP
  • June 2005 - Supreme Court confirms Cable Internet as unregulated
  • Sept 2005 - DSL deregulated
  • Sept 2005 - FCC "Internet Pulicy Statement" their first Net Neutrality
  • Nov 2005 - AT&T "Anybody who expects to use pipes for free is nuts!"
  • May 2006 - Senate considers a bill on Net Neutality

Timeline

2006-2007

  • June 2006 - "Series of Tubes"
  • Feb 2007 - Skype any device on wireless networks
  • Sept-Nov 2007 - Comcast Blocks Bittorrent
  • Oct 2007 - "Delays bittorrent"
  • Nov 2007 - FCC complaint filed about Comcast and bittorrent

Timeline

2008

  • Jan 2008 - FCC investigates
  • Apr 2008 - FCC tells Skype, wireless is wierd
  • Aug 2008 - FCC tells Comcast to stop "delaying" bittorrent
  • Sept 2008 - Comcast appeals
  • Nov 2008 - President elected with a pro Net Neutrality talking point

Timeline

2009-2010

  • Oct 2009 - FCC starts work on Open Internet Order
  • Apr 2010 - Comcast Wins - Title I doesn't apply to cable
  • May 2010 - FCC okay let's make broadband a telecomm service
  • Aug 2010 - Google and Verzion WAT!
  • Dec 2010 - FCC issues Open Internet Order

Timeline

2011-2013

  • Sep 2011 - FCC published Open Internet Order
  • Sep 2011 - Verzion challenges the order
  • Sep 2012 - AT&T blocking facetime challenged under order
  • Jan 2013 - AT&T unblocks facetime (on their own)
  • Sept 2013 - Verizon and FCC in court
  • Sept 2013 - Verizon "Open Internet rules only thing from preventing from charging websites for customers"

Timeline

2014 (part 1)

  • Jan 2014 - AT&T announces sponsored data
  • Jan 2014 - Court tells FCC again they are not telecomm
  • Feb 2014 - FCC explores Section 706 leaves Title II on docket
  • Apr 2014 - FCC Proposal leaked (fast lanes)
  • May 2014 - FCC Proposal released (fast lanes, title II?)

Timeline

2014 (part 2)

  • Jun 2014 - T-Mobile Music Streaming Exemption
  • Jul 2014 - Big Tech speaks out about Net Neuttrality

Timeline

2014 (part 3)

  • Aug 2014 - Obama stands against fast lanes

  • Sept 2014 - Congress urges FCC to reclassify
